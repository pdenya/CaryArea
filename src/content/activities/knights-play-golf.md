A fully illuminated 27-hole par-three course plus a 60-station driving range, open until midnight. This is the evening activity that solves the heat problem completely — you play after dark when it's actually pleasant outside, and the lit-up course is genuinely fun, not just a compromise.

Par-three means every hole is short enough for the kids, and the teens can still compete. Way more interesting than regular mini golf, but just as accessible.

**Know before you go**

- 2512 Ten Ten Rd, Apex
- Open until midnight — go after sunset
- 27 holes, all par-three (short and approachable)
- 60-station driving range if you just want to hit balls
- ~$10–15 per person
