Pullen Park is Raleigh's old-fashioned amusement park in miniature: a vintage carousel, a little train that loops the grounds, and paddle boats on the pond. At roughly $1.50 a ride, you can let every kid ride everything twice and still spend less than a single arcade card. The nine-year-old and the eleven-year-olds are the sweet spot, but nobody's too cool for the paddle boats.

The heat-wave trick is in the timing: go after 6pm, when the sun softens and dusk settles over the pond. That's when this park is at its best anyway — golden light, cooler air, and a carousel spinning as the day winds down. An easy, cheap, low-planning evening.

**Know before you go**
- Rides ~$1.50 each — carousel, train, paddle boats
- Go after 6pm — dusk is the whole point in this heat
