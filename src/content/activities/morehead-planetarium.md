Ask local parents where to take kids and Morehead Planetarium comes up constantly — and on a scorcher it's close to genius. You're trading a punishing July afternoon for a dark, cold dome full of stars, which is exactly the kind of trade this week is about. The younger kids get an actual wow moment; the teens get a legitimately cool show instead of a kiddie outing.

The reason it makes the list as a full afternoon, though, is what's outside the door: Franklin Street. Do the planetarium, then wander Chapel Hill's main drag for a food and boba crawl afterward, and you've built an entire AC-powered afternoon out of one drive.

**Know before you go**

- Chapel Hill, right by Franklin Street
- A recurring local favorite for kids
- The play: show first, then the Franklin St food and boba crawl for a full AC afternoon
