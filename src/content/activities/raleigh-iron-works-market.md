Send-off Saturday has options, and this one costs nothing: the Market at Raleigh Iron Works runs 11am to 4pm on July 11. It's a free wander — vendors, browsing, and something for every age in the group, from the youngest kids to the teens who'd rather not admit they're having fun.

It made the list because a send-off Saturday at the end of a visit week wants low-commitment, drop-in stuff. This pairs naturally with the Car Showcase at Dix Park the same day (10am–2pm) if you want to string together a Raleigh morning-into-afternoon before the send-off dinner.

**Know before you go**

- Sat Jul 11, 11am–4pm, free
- Same-day combo: Car Showcase at Dix Park runs 10am–2pm
- Midday timing in a heat wave — hats, water bottles, and sunscreen for everyone
