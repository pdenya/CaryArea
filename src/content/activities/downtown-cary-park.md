If the week needs a default evening plan, this is it. Downtown Cary Park has something for every single member of your crew: a splash pad and a shaded Kidzone with giant cardinal structures for the younger two, ping-pong tables for the teens, and the Bark Bar pouring drinks for the adults while food trucks handle dinner. Nobody has to compromise, and nobody has to pay admission.

Even better for your week specifically: the park runs free programming nearly every day, so there's a decent chance you stumble into live music or an event without planning a thing. Check downtowncarypark.com/events before you head over. Save it for the evening hours, when the splash pad does double duty as heat relief.

**Know before you go**
- Free, open roughly 7am–10pm
- 327 S Academy St, Cary
- Free events nearly every day that week — check downtowncarypark.com/events
- Splash pad, shaded Kidzone, Bark Bar, food trucks, ping-pong
