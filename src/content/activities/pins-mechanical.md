Duckpin bowling — the smaller balls, no shoes required version — plus pinball and games at Seaboard Station in Raleigh. It's more casual and more fun than regular bowling for a mixed-age group.

Verify it's open before heading over. Partee Shack in North Raleigh is a backup option.

**Know before you go**

- Seaboard Station, downtown Raleigh
- Duckpin = smaller balls, no shoe rental needed
- Verify hours/availability before going
- Family-friendly during daytime hours
