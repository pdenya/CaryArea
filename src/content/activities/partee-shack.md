ParTee Shack opened their third location in North Raleigh in April 2025 with mini golf, duckpin bowling with light shows, VR laser tag, and a full arcade. It's a solid alternative to Frankie's — newer, less hectic, and the duckpin bowling is a fun twist.

**Know before you go**

- 6231 Triangle Plantation Dr, Raleigh
- Mon–Thu 10am–9pm, Fri–Sat 10am–11pm, Sun 1–8pm
- Multiple activities make it easy to fill a few hours in the AC
