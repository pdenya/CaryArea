Two experiences at one venue just off I-40, about 15 minutes from Cary. Puttery is upscale indoor mini golf — two 9-hole courses, $15 adults / $12 kids, themed rooms with good music and lighting. The driving range side has Trackman technology for swing data nerds.

The mini golf is a step up from the typical blacklight course. Good for a mixed group — competitive enough for teens, approachable for the younger kids.

**Know before you go**

- 6901 Play Golf Way, Raleigh (15 min from Cary, off I-40)
- Puttery: $15 adults / $12 kids for indoor mini golf
- Driving range with Trackman tech
- Full AC
