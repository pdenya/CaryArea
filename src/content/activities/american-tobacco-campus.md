The old American Tobacco factory is now Durham's best free wander: brick warehouses, the iconic Lucky Strike smokestack, and a man-made river walk threading through the middle of it all. Kids follow the water, adults soak up the architecture, and when anyone gets hungry there are around fifteen restaurants on the campus — dinner solves itself.

This one is built for the evening slot in your heat-wave schedule. Roll in as the sun drops, stroll the river walk, then let the group split up for food. Before you go, check americantobacco.co/events — the campus hosts free concerts, and catching one turns a nice stroll into a full night out.

**Know before you go**
- Free to wander; ~15 restaurants on campus for dinner
- River walk and the Lucky Strike smokestack are the highlights
- Check americantobacco.co/events for free concerts
- Evening stroll — not a midday stop in this heat
