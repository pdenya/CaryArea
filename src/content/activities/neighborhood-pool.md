Not every heat-wave rescue needs a reservation, a drive, or a game plan. Your neighborhood or community pool is the staycation's secret weapon: it's close by, it costs nothing, and nobody has to pack anything more complicated than a towel and sunscreen.

This is the move for a lazy first day, for the morning after a big outing, or for any afternoon when the group can't agree on anything except "water, now." The younger kids can splash for hours, the teens can float and pretend they're too cool for it, and the adults get actual chairs in actual shade. When the forecast says brutal all week, having a zero-planning default in your back pocket is what keeps the whole week relaxed instead of scheduled to death.
