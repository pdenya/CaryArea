Lawrence Barbecue opened in downtown Cary in September 2025, bringing serious craft BBQ right to your backyard. Brisket, ribs, pulled pork, plus oysters and a solid drink menu. It's a step up from a typical BBQ joint without being fussy.

Walking distance from Downtown Cary Park — easy combo: BBQ for dinner, then walk to the park for the splash pad and evening hangout.

**Know before you go**

- 150 E Cedar St, Cary (downtown)
- Wed–Thu 11am–9pm, Fri–Sat 11am–10pm, closed Mon–Tue
- Pair with Downtown Cary Park (5 min walk)
