When Reddit locals talk about Chinese food in the Triangle, this is the name that keeps coming up — "best Chinese food in the region," and it's sitting right in Cary. That makes it less a restaurant recommendation and more a food pilgrimage you barely have to drive for. The dish to order: the Chong Qing chicken.

For a week built around dodging the sun, a serious sit-down Chinese dinner minutes away is an easy win — no planning, full AC, and food good enough that your guests go home talking about it.

**Know before you go**

- The Chong Qing chicken is the dish that comes up by name
- Avoid Friday and Saturday — go on a weeknight instead
- It's in Cary, so this is one of the closest picks on the list
