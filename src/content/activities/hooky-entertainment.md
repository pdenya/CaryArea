Hooky (formerly EVO) is a massive entertainment complex right in Cary with bowling, dine-in movies in luxury recliners, a big arcade with redemption prizes, billiards, darts, foosball, air hockey, shuffleboard, and sports simulators. Opened summer 2025.

This is one of those places where you walk in for bowling and don't leave for four hours. The dine-in movie theater is a legit alternative to CMX CinéBistro, and the arcade keeps the younger kids busy while the teens bowl.

**Know before you go**

- 1140 Parkside Main St, Cary (right by Flour Power and Spare Time's old spot)
- Open daily 10am–midnight
- Multiple activities = easy half-day in the AC
- Full food and drink menu
