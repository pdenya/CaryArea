When the afternoon sun turns brutal, this is the escape hatch: four full floors of air-conditioned exhibits in downtown Raleigh, and the general admission price is exactly zero. That makes it one of the easiest wins of the whole week — no tickets to book, no budget math, just load up the crew and wander until everyone's cooled off and full of dinosaur facts.

There's a bonus for anyone who wants a little extra: the "Staying Alive" special exhibit is running through August. That one's ticketed, but the rest of the museum stays free, so you can mix and match depending on the group's energy.

**Know before you go**

- General admission is free — four floors of AC
- "Staying Alive" special exhibit: ~$10–15, running through August
- A perfect peak-heat (12–5pm) afternoon pick
