Botiwalla just opened June 2, 2026 at Raleigh Iron Works — Indian street food from the Chai Pani Restaurant Group, which won the James Beard Award for "most outstanding restaurant in America." Okra fries, lamb burgers, kebab rolls, rice bowls, and chaats. Casual counter-service format that works great with kids.

The Iron Works complex is becoming a destination — NOCO (Bond Brothers' new brewery), Cannonball Music Hall, and more are all here now.

**Know before you go**

- 2221 Iron Works Dr, Suite 120, Raleigh
- Sun–Thu 11am–9pm, Fri–Sat 11am–10pm
- Counter-service = fast and kid-friendly
- Pair with the Sat 7/11 Raleigh Iron Works Market if timing works
