Actual lemurs, right up the road in Durham — this is the "wait, we can do WHAT here?" pick of the week, and the one the kids will still be talking about long after the week is over. Standard tours run $12–17 per person, and if you want to go all in, the Walking with Lemurs experience puts you right in there with them for $85 (ages 10+, so tweens and up make the cut).

Here's the hard truth: July sells out. Reservations are prepaid and required, so if anyone in the group lights up at the word "lemur," book immediately — this is a decide-today item, not a wing-it item.

**Know before you go**

- Tours: $12–17 per person; Walking with Lemurs: $85, ages 10+
- Prepaid reservation required — no walk-ups
- July sells out — book NOW if interested
