This is the paddle that will make everyone ask, "wait, this is here?" Robertson Millpond Preserve in Wendell puts you on a blackwater cypress swamp — dark still water, cypress trees rising straight out of it — a landscape that feels smuggled in from somewhere far more exotic than Wake County.

Two things make it a heat-wave gem. First, it's shaded: you're paddling under the cypress canopy instead of frying on open water, which changes everything on a scorcher. Second, it's genuinely memorable — of all the water outings on this list, this is the one that doubles as a "whoa" moment for your guests. Rentals are available on-site on weekends, so pencil it in for Saturday and let the swamp do the showing off.

**Know before you go**

- Shaded blackwater cypress swamp paddling
- On-site rentals on weekends
- Located in Wendell
