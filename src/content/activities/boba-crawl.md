Iced drinks with chewy pearls, ordered in bulk by a pack of kids and teens — boba was made for a heat wave. This crawl strings together three spots around Cary and Morrisville.

Start at Boba Baba in Morrisville, which earns its place with more than drinks: ube puffs and mochi donuts, the kind of snacks the kids will be photographing before eating. Then Milk Lab in Cary for round two. Finish at Cha House, also in Cary, where the move is boba plus popcorn chicken — because by the third stop, somebody's going to want actual food. Three stops, all air-conditioned, all a short drive apart. Do them in one ambitious afternoon or spread them across the week as your default cool-down errand.

**Know before you go**

- Boba Baba (Morrisville): get the ube puffs and mochi donuts
- Milk Lab (Cary): the middle stop
- Cha House (Cary): add the popcorn chicken
