Any movie theater beats the heat, but only one theater in the entire state can do this: the Wells Fargo IMAX in Raleigh is North Carolina's only 3D-capable giant screen. When the teens start rolling their eyes at kid-forward activities, this is your card to play — it comes locally teen-approved, and a screen that big impresses every age anyway.

It slots neatly into the peak-heat window: a couple of hours in a cold, dark dome-sized theater while the sun does its worst outside. Check what's showing during the visit week and match the film to your crowd — a big documentary or blockbuster on that screen is an event either way.

**Know before you go**

- Located in Raleigh
- The only 3D-capable giant screen in NC — worth choosing over a regular theater for the spectacle
- Teen-approved per local recommendations
