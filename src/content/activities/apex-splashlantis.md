Splashlantis earns its slightly ridiculous name. It's a free splash pad at Apex's Pleasant Park, and it comes with a bonus most splash pads can't touch: a giant hillside slide that the kids will run up and fly down until you physically remove them.

That combo is what puts it above the average sprinkler park. The splash pad handles the cooling-off, the slide handles the thrill-seeking, and the price handles the family budget — this whole outing costs exactly nothing. On a week where the big-ticket water days (quarry lakes, water parks, pontoons) are doing the heavy lifting, Splashlantis is the perfect in-between: a low-effort, high-delight stop that keeps the crew wet and happy without a single ticket, waiver, or reservation.

**Know before you go**

- Completely free
- Splash pad plus the giant hillside slide
- Details at apexnc.org/splash
