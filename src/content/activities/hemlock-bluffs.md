This is your zero-effort escape hatch: right in Cary, just minutes away, and, as locals put it, "completely shaded... like someone dropped a chunk of mountains right in Cary." The trails top out around 3 miles total, much of it on boardwalks, so even younger kids can do the whole thing without complaint.

Because it's so close and so easy, it's the perfect evening option — head over after 5:30pm when the day cools, or grab the 8–9:30am window if you want a morning leg-stretcher before the heat builds. One thing to set expectations on with the kids: there's no creek play here, so save the water shoes for the Eno.

**Know before you go**

- ~3 miles of trail max, with boardwalks
- Free; just a 10-minute drive within Cary
- No creek play — this one's shade only
- Great easy evening option
- Do tick checks afterward
