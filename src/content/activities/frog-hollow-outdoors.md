Every other item on this list is dodging the sun. This one skips the sun entirely. Frog Hollow Outdoors in Durham runs a kayak-under-the-stars outing with a Morehead astronomer along for the ride — you paddle out in the dark, look up, and get the night sky narrated by someone who actually knows what you're looking at.

An evening paddle is the ultimate heat-wave loophole: by the time you're on the water, the brutal part of the day is over, and the whole outing is effectively heat-proof. It's also the pick most likely to become the story of the week — kayaks, starlight, and a real astronomer is a hard combination to top. If a stargazing night doesn't line up, Frog Hollow also does regular rentals on the Eno and Falls Lake.

**Know before you go**

- Kayak under the stars with a Morehead astronomer
- Evening timing makes it effectively heat-proof
- Regular Eno and Falls Lake rentals also available
