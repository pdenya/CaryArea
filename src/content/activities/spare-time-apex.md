When the group can't agree on one activity, take them somewhere with three. Spare Time Apex packs a two-story laser tag arena, bowling lanes, and an arcade under a single roof — which means the teens and the younger kids can all find their thing without anyone splitting off in a huff.

The two-story laser tag arena is the headliner here, and it's a great equalizer: a tween with quick feet can absolutely humble an adult. Bowl a game, run a laser tag match or two, burn some arcade credits, and you've filled an entire brutal-heat afternoon without stepping outside.

**Know before you go**

- Laser tag, bowling, and arcade all in one building — easy to mix and match
- It's close by in Apex, so it works as a low-effort fallback plan
