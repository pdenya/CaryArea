A dedicated ninja warrior training gym in Apex — not just a section of a trampoline park, but a full facility with real obstacles. Open gym sessions, camps, and birthday parties. The parent lounge has WiFi, so you can sit and work (or not) while the kids exhaust themselves.

Great equalizer across ages — the 9-year-old and 17-year-old are both challenged but in different ways.

**Know before you go**

- 2131 E Williams St, Apex
- Open gym sessions available
- Parent lounge with WiFi
- Check schedule for open gym times
