A working chocolate factory in downtown Raleigh where you can just... walk in, watch them make chocolate, and taste it — for free. Videri's self-guided factory viewing is one of those finds that sounds too good to be real, and it's air-conditioned on top of it, which makes it a perfect little AC pit stop on any downtown Raleigh day.

It's not a whole afternoon on its own, but that's the charm: stack it with Marbles, the Natural Sciences museum, or an evening downtown, and it becomes the sweet interlude everyone remembers. Adults get their moment too — Black & White coffee is right next door.

**Know before you go**

- Free self-guided factory viewing, tasting included
- Air-conditioned — an easy cool-down stop
- Black & White coffee is next door for the grown-ups
