Sometimes the answer to "it's a hundred degrees and the kids are melting" doesn't need to be an event. Jack Smith Park's splash pad is free, it's right in Cary, and it's tailor-made for younger kids — they can sprint through the jets until they're waterlogged while the adults supervise from whatever shade they can claim.

It made the list precisely because it asks nothing of you: no tickets, no drive across the Triangle, no booking window to stress over. It's the low-stakes option for a slow morning, a between-plans gap, or a quick cool-down before dinner. Bring towels, bring water bottles, and let the splash pad do the heavy lifting on a day when the heat has everyone's fuse running short.
