Durham's Carolina Theatre runs Free Retro Family Days in July — free family films on select Saturdays in a grand old theater with serious air conditioning. If the schedule includes Saturday July 11, it's one of the best peak-heat moves of the whole week: seven people, a classic movie, a beautiful room, zero dollars.

It made the list as a Saturday afternoon ace-in-the-hole. The morning is already spoken for by farmers markets or the Dix Park car showcase, and this fills the brutal 12–5pm window without anyone melting or the budget flinching. Verify the date at carolinatheatre.org before you build the day around it.

**Know before you go**

- Free family films on select July Saturdays — confirm Jul 11 at carolinatheatre.org
- Fully air-conditioned — ideal for the peak-heat afternoon block
- Pairs with a Durham morning: farmers market at Central Park, treats at The Parlour
