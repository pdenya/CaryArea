Trampolines, dodgeball courts, and a ninja warrior course under one roof in Cary. It's a full-body-tired kind of afternoon — the kids will sleep well after this one.

Another trampoline park option alongside DEFY and Urban Air, but this one's in Cary proper, so it's the closest to home.

**Know before you go**

- 220 Grande Heights Dr, Cary
- ~$20–30 per person
- Trampolines, dodgeball, ninja warrior
- Grip socks required (available for purchase)
