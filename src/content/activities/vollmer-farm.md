If you want berry picking that's actually likely to have berries in mid-July, Vollmer Farm out in Bunn is your best bet: their blueberries and blackberries run through July 31, which puts your visit comfortably inside the window instead of gambling on the last stragglers. Two kinds of fruit also means twice the picking entertainment for the kids.

The trade-off is the drive — about 45 minutes each way — so treat this as a proper morning outing. Leave early, pick while it's still bearable, and you're back home before the afternoon furnace kicks in. As with any u-pick at the tail of the season, a quick call ahead saves you a wasted trip.

**Know before you go**
- Blueberries and blackberries through Jul 31 — the best mid-July bet
- ~45 minutes from Cary, in Bunn
- Call first — mid-July is the tail of the season
- Go in the morning, before the heat peaks
