Korean BBQ is dinner as a group activity: meat sizzling on a grill in the middle of your own table, everyone reaching in, nobody stuck with a boring plate. Five Spice in Cary does it all-you-can-eat, which is the right format for a big group with teenagers in it — nobody has to negotiate over portions, and the hungry ones can just keep going.

Best part for a mixed-ages table: the staff will handle the cooking, so you're not deputizing a kid as grill master. It's kid-workable, right in Cary, and thoroughly air-conditioned — a solid big-group dinner on a scorching evening.

**Know before you go**

- All-you-can-eat, grill-at-table format
- Staff will do the cooking for you — kid-workable
- Figure ~$30–40 per person
