About 45 minutes up the road in Henderson, the Raleigh Road Outdoor Theatre is the classic evening move: a real drive-in showing a double feature under the summer sky. Pile everyone into the car, load up on snacks, and let the kids sprawl out — it's the rare outing where grade-schoolers and teens are all equally on board.

It made the list because evenings are the golden hours of this heat-wave week, and a drive-in doesn't even start until the sun is gone. A double feature also turns one night into a full event — the second movie is where the memories (and the kids falling asleep in the back seat) happen.

**Know before you go**

- Check the marquee first — the lineup decides whether the night works for your crew
- Double feature means a late night; plan a slow next morning
- About 45 minutes each way, so commit to the full evening
