Not every afternoon needs to be trampolines and go-karts. When the group needs to dial it down a notch, the Nasher at Duke is free, thoroughly air-conditioned, and genuinely kid-tolerant for ages 8 and up — which covers every kid in your crew. Wander the galleries at whatever pace the day demands, then land at the nice café when legs get tired.

It slots neatly into a Durham day: pair it with a morning at Duke Gardens or an evening stroll past Duke Chapel, and you've built a full outing where the hottest hours happen entirely indoors.

**Know before you go**

- Free admission, full AC
- Open Tue–Sat 10–5, Thursdays until 9
- Best for ages 8+ — the whole crew qualifies
- Nice café on site for a snack stop
