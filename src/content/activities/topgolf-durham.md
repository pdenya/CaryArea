Topgolf is the rare "outdoor" activity that survives a heat wave: the hitting bays are shaded and fitted with fans, and everyone from the nine-year-old to the grown-ups can whack balls at glowing targets without knowing a thing about golf. One bay holds six players, so your crew of seven or eight only needs two bays side by side.

Here's the move that made it list-worthy: it's half-off Monday through Thursday when you book online, and bays start around $20 an hour. Book ahead, grab a weekday slot, and you've got a genuinely cheap group outing. It sits right next to Streets at Southpoint, so you can pair it with an air-conditioned mall wander.

**Know before you go**
- Bays from ~$20/hr, up to 6 players per bay
- Half-off Mon–Thu — but only if you book online
- Shaded bays with fans, though still aim for morning or evening
- 4901 Topgolf Way, right next to Southpoint
