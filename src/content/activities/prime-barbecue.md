If the week needs one proper North Carolina barbecue meal — and with out-of-town family visiting, it does — this is the one. Prime Barbecue in Knightdale is the Triangle's most-loved BBQ, the answer locals give when someone asks where to take visitors. It's about 30 minutes from Cary, which makes it an easy lunch-or-dinner run rather than an expedition.

Feeling more ambitious? Sam Jones in Winterville is the whole-hog pilgrimage — but that's an hour and a half each way, so save it for a trip built around it. For this week, with this heat and this crew, Prime is the move.

**Know before you go**

- About 30 minutes from Cary
- Triangle's most-loved BBQ — the local consensus pick
- Sam Jones (Winterville, 1.5 hr) is the whole-hog pilgrimage option if you want to go big
