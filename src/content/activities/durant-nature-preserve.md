This is the sleeper pick for the kid contingent. Durant combines the two things every child actually wants from a "hike": creek play and a playground — and the playground is shaded, which in a week like this one is worth its weight in popsicles. The kids can splash and climb while the adults claim a shady spot nearby, making it more of a hangout with trees than a march through them.

Keep to the golden hours all the same: arrive between 8 and 9:30am, or swing by after 5:30pm for an easy evening outing. Pack water shoes, a change of clothes, and zero ambition — that's the whole point.

**Know before you go**

- Free
- Creek play plus a shaded playground — bring water shoes and dry clothes
- Easiest as a morning or evening trip in this heat
- Do tick checks afterward
