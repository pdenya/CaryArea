This is the night that makes the whole beach overnight write itself. Every Thursday in summer, Carolina Beach throws Boardwalk Blast: live music on the boardwalk from 6:30 to 9:30pm, capped by free fireworks over the ocean at 9pm. Thursday July 9 lands right in the middle of your week, so the timing could not be better.

It's the built-in evening anchor that put Carolina Beach ahead of Wrightsville and Atlantic Beach for the overnight trip. The kids ride boardwalk rides and hit the arcade while the band plays, then everyone watches the sky light up over the water — no tickets, no planning, just show up.

**Know before you go**

- Music runs 6:30–9:30pm; fireworks at 9pm — both free
- Boardwalk rides and arcade are right there for before and between
- Parking is paid (~$5/hr) but more available than Wrightsville
- Pairs with the Carolina Beach overnight — see that plan for lodging and the drive
