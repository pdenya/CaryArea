When the forecast is this brutal, dessert stops being a treat and becomes a survival strategy. Cary's two homegrown stops make it easy. First: Andia's Ice Cream — get the baklava flavor, full stop. That's the order. There are two Andia's shops in Cary, so one is never far from wherever the day has taken you.

Then there's Goodberry's, the local frozen custard standby — a different animal from ice cream and a Cary rite of passage for the visiting cousins. You don't have to hit both in one day (though nobody's stopping you); think of this as your rotating end-of-day cooldown for the whole week.

**Know before you go**

- Andia's: the baklava flavor is the one to get
- Two Andia's locations in Cary — pick whichever is closer
- Goodberry's is frozen custard — the local classic
