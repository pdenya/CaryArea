Over 2,000 birds from around the world, and the draw is hands-on: kids hand-feed flamingos and parakeets. It's a longer drive (~1.5 hours) but it's genuinely unique — there's nothing else like it in the region.

Tue–Sun 9–5, mostly outdoor. Start early to get ahead of the heat and plan a full morning there.

**Know before you go**

- ~1.5 hours from Cary (Scotland Neck)
- Tue–Sun 9–5
- Mostly outdoor — arrive at opening
- Hand-feeding is the highlight, especially for the younger kids
