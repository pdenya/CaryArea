Somewhere between "neighborhood pool" and "full water park" sits Buffaloe Road, and it hits the sweet spot for a mixed-age crew. Three slides give the kids (and, let's be honest, the teens) something to race each other on, and the lazy river gives everyone else a reason to just float and let the week happen.

At roughly $5–8 a head, it's way more fun than a plain pool for barely more money — the kind of value that makes it easy to say yes even on a day you didn't plan anything. Open Monday through Saturday, 10 to 7, so it works as a peak-afternoon escape when the sun is doing its worst and the schedule says "water or AC, nothing else."

**Know before you go**

- ~$5–8 per person
- Open Mon–Sat, 10am–7pm
- Three slides plus a lazy river
- Way more fun than a plain pool for the price
