This just opened in June 2025 and it's a genuinely world-class playground — 18.5 acres at Dorothea Dix Park with a waterfall, climbing towers, slides, a massive sand bowl, sensory maze, swing terrace, play gardens, and a splash pad. Largest adventure playground in the Southeast. And it's completely free.

Pair this with the Dix Park Car Showcase on Sat 7/11 — they're in the same park. Go early morning before it gets brutal, or evening when it cools off.

**Know before you go**

- FREE admission and FREE parking
- Open daily 8am–9pm
- Lake Wheeler Road entrance
- Splash pad means bring towels and a change of clothes
- Shade is limited in some areas — go morning or evening in July
- Combine with the Thomas Dambo troll hunt in the same park
