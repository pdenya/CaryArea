Saturday July 11 brings a Car Showcase to Dix Park from 10am to 2pm, and it earned a spot on the shortlist for the last full day of the visit — it's even penciled into the rough plan as the Saturday morning option instead of pool time.

If anyone in your crew lights up around cars (and the research notes suggest someone does), this is an easy morning: wander the rows, pick favorites, argue about them over lunch. It also stacks neatly with the free Market at Raleigh Iron Works, which runs 11am–4pm the same day, so you can turn Saturday into a two-stop Raleigh outing before the send-off dinner.

**Know before you go**

- Sat Jul 11, 10am–2pm at Dix Park
- Go early — it's an outdoor event in a heat wave, and mornings are the plan all week
- Pairs with the Market at Raleigh Iron Works (11am–4pm) for a full Saturday
- Sunscreen, hats, and water bottles — the standing rule this week
