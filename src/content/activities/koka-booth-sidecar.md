Friday night, right in Cary: Sidecar Social Club brings swing and jazz to Koka Booth Amphitheatre at 8pm, and lawn tickets are cheap. By showtime the worst of the day's heat has broken, so you can sprawl on blankets while the band plays and the little kids run out their last energy of the day.

It slots perfectly into the week — you'll be rolling back from the beach overnight Friday afternoon, and this is a zero-effort landing: no long drive, no big spend, easy exit whenever the youngest kids fade. Fair warning, it competes with the free "Wicked: For Good" outdoor movie at Moore Square the same night, so pick your Friday.

**Know before you go**

- Fri Jul 10, 8pm — an evening show, so the heat has eased
- Cheap lawn tickets; bring blankets or low chairs
- Right in Cary — an easy optional add-on after the beach return
- Same-night rival: the free Wicked outdoor movie at Moore Square
