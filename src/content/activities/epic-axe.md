Every staycation week needs one outing that's just for the big kids, and Epic Axe in Raleigh is it: hurling axes at wooden targets in air-conditioned comfort while the younger crowd does something else entirely. It's peak teens-and-adults energy, and exactly the kind of thing the teens and the grown-ups will get competitive about fast.

The one heads-up: Epic Axe is strictly ages 12 and up, so any under-12s in your group miss the cut here. If the goal is for everyone to throw, TreeRunner's axe range takes throwers from age 10 — but if you're deliberately splitting the group for an afternoon, this is the marquee older-crew pick.

**Know before you go**

- Ages 12+ only — plan it as a teens-and-adults outing
- Alternative: TreeRunner's axe range allows ages 10+ if the whole crew wants in
