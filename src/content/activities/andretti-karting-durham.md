Andretti Indoor Karting & Games just opened in May 2026 — the Triangle's newest and biggest indoor entertainment venue. Named after Mario Andretti, this 90,000 sq ft facility is right next to Topgolf Durham at Southpoint, so you could easily pair them.

Multi-level electric go-kart track with high-speed Superkarts, plus a two-story laser tag arena, Hologate VR, Hyperdeck VR, professional racing simulators, duckpin bowling, a 7D motion theater, and a huge arcade. This place is basically an entire afternoon under one roof.

**Know before you go**

- Mini Mario karts for ages 3–6 (must be 36"+ tall)
- Regular karts require 42"+ height
- Right next to Topgolf Durham — easy combo trip
- Full restaurant and bar on-site
- Brand new (opened May 19, 2026) — everything is fresh
- Book online to avoid waits, especially on weekends
