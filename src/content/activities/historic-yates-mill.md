A genuinely pretty 1756 gristmill sitting over its pond — the kind of postcard scene that makes everyone's phone come out. It's free, and the visitor center gives you an air-conditioned home base, so you can do the outdoor part in a cool morning or evening window and then duck inside when the heat catches up.

One honest caveat: the mill tours with the working demo run July 21–22, after your crew has flown home, so this visit is about the scenery and the visitor center rather than seeing the mill grind. That still makes it a lovely low-effort outing — just set expectations accordingly.

**Know before you go**

- Free, with a free air-conditioned visitor center
- Outdoor grounds — go morning or evening in this heat
- Mill tours are Jul 21–22, so you'd miss the demo this week
