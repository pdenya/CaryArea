The NC Courage host the Washington Spirit on Saturday evening at WakeMed Soccer Park — right in Cary, minutes from home. It's Ring of Honor night, which adds some extra pageantry. The 6:30pm kickoff means you're watching under lights as the heat breaks.

Pro soccer with a party of 7 is easy: the venue is built for families, parking is straightforward, and $15–40 a ticket is reasonable for a big-group outing.

**Know before you go**

- WakeMed Soccer Park, Cary — very close to home
- 6:30pm kickoff, Saturday July 11
- ~$15–40 per ticket
- NCFC is not fielding a team in 2026, so this is the Triangle's pro soccer option
