Reddit's verdict is simple: "best biscuits in Raleigh." The State Farmers Market Restaurant sits right at the NC State Farmers Market, which turns breakfast into a two-part outing — pile everyone in, eat biscuits until you can't, then walk it off browsing the market stalls next door.

In a week where afternoons are off-limits, this is the ideal morning play: you're out early, fed well, and wandering the market before the heat peaks. Great with visiting family too — a farmers market full of North Carolina produce is a better postcard of the area than any mall.

**Know before you go**

- The biscuits are the headline — come hungry
- Walk the farmers market after breakfast
- Plan it as a morning outing, before the heat gets brutal
