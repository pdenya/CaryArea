Finding a restaurant that genuinely handles a big group — without splitting you across two booths or making you wait an hour — is half the battle on a visit week. Superica, the Ford Fry Tex-Mex spot at Fenton, handles big tables, and Tex-Mex is about as crowd-proof a cuisine as exists: something on the menu for the kids, the teens, and the adults alike.

Bonus: it puts you at Fenton, so you can tack on a post-dinner wander — Honeysuckle Gelato and the bookstore are right there if anyone's still got room or energy.

**Know before you go**

- Handles big tables — good fit for a large group
- Figure ~$20–35 per person
- It's at Fenton, so dessert and a stroll are steps away
