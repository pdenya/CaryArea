This Durham favorite packs a butterfly house, bears, lemurs, and a ride-on train into one campus, plus the new "From Here On" exhibit — there's genuinely something here for the younger kids and the teens alike. It's the kind of place where a "quick visit" turns into a full morning without anyone complaining.

The catch during a heat wave: roughly half of it is outdoors, including the animal areas. The move is to show up right at opening, knock out the outdoor loop while it's still bearable, then retreat into the air-conditioned indoor exhibits as the day heats up.

**Know before you go**

- Admission runs ~$23–25
- Half the museum is outdoor — arrive at opening and do outside first
- New "From Here On" exhibit is on now
