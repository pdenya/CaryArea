DPAC has two heavy hitters that weekend: Blackberry Smoke on Friday July 10 and An Evening with Wilco on Saturday July 11, both at 8pm with tickets around $40 and up. This is prime teen-guitarist bait — if your teens have opinions about guitars, this is their lane.

It's not a whole-family outing at that price, but as a split-the-group night it's a gem: a couple of adults plus a teen or two in a fully air-conditioned theater while everyone else does the free stuff. Friday is tight against the beach-overnight return and the Koka Booth show, so Saturday's Wilco night may be the cleaner fit.

**Know before you go**

- Blackberry Smoke Fri Jul 10; An Evening with Wilco Sat Jul 11 — both 8pm
- Tickets roughly $40 and up
- Indoor and air-conditioned — heat-proof by design
- Friday competes with the beach return and two free events; Saturday is more open
