If the crew loves the Jordan Lake idea but you want the deluxe version, Seaforth is the answer. It beats Ebenezer on nearly every count: a bigger beach, a shaded picnic grove for the adults to actually survive in, loaner life jackets for the kids, and fewer crowds fighting you for towel space.

That shaded grove is the sleeper feature during a heat wave — it means the grown-ups can hold down a home base out of the sun while the kids run the beach-to-water loop all morning. One bit of homework before you load the car: check the NC DEQ swim advisories, especially after rain, so you know the water's good to go.

**Know before you go**

- Bigger beach and fewer crowds than Ebenezer Church Access
- Shaded picnic grove — rare and precious in this heat
- Loaner life jackets available
- Check NC DEQ swim advisories after rain
