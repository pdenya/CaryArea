Local Redditors crowned this one the coldest room in the Triangle, and in a week like this, that's the highest compliment an activity can get. The Sportsplex in Hillsborough gives you a full ice rink's worth of chill — the kind of cold you'll actually be grateful for by Wednesday afternoon.

What earns it a spot over closer rinks is the combo move: pair the skate with a stop at Eno River Farm for ice cream afterward. That pairing pulled 13 upvotes from locals on its own — cold rink, cold scoop, one very smug family. It turns a rink visit into a proper Hillsborough half-day.

**Know before you go**

- Located in Hillsborough — treat it as a half-day trip with the ice cream stop
- The local-approved pairing: skate first, then Eno River Farm for ice cream
- The coldest rink option in the Triangle per locals — bring a layer
