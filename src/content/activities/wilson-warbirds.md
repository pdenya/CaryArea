The Mudcats relocated to Wilson and became the Warbirds — new stadium, new name, same affordable minor league ball. They're home the entire week against the Fayetteville Woodpeckers, so you can slot a game into almost any evening.

Friday is the night to aim for if you can only go once — fireworks are the typical Friday promo. Evening games mean you dodge the worst of the heat, and minor league parks are designed for families: cheap tickets, easy parking, mascot races between innings.

**Know before you go**

- About 50 minutes from Cary
- Check the promo schedule for fireworks and theme nights
- Evening start times work perfectly with the heat strategy
