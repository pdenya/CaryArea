The NC Transportation Museum in Spencer has a giant roundhouse, train rides, and a lot of shaded indoor space. The timing is interesting: **Summer Fun Day is Tuesday July 7** — special caboose rides on arrival day. Regular train rides run Thursday, Friday, and Saturday too.

It's ~1.5 hours away, so it's a commitment, but the giant roundhouse is mostly shaded/indoor and the train rides are a guaranteed hit with the younger kids.

**Know before you go**

- ~1.5 hours from Cary (Spencer)
- Summer Fun Day with special caboose rides on Tue 7/7
- Regular train rides Thu/Fri/Sat
- $16–20 per person plus admission
- Giant roundhouse is shaded/indoor — good heat refuge
