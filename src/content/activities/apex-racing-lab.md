This one was the surprise of the whole research binge: a full-on racing simulator lab hiding in MacGregor Village, right in Cary. Twenty pro-grade motion sim rigs — the kind that tilt and shove you through every corner — with instructors on hand to get everyone up to speed.

It's tailor-made for a mixed-age crew. Picture the gearheads in the group lined up on adjacent rigs, racing head-to-head while the heat wave rages outside and you don't feel a degree of it. It's competitive enough for the teens, approachable enough for the younger kids, and unapologetic dad bait.

**Know before you go**

- Sessions run roughly $30–60
- Book ahead — don't just show up
- It's in Cary (MacGregor Village), so it slots easily into any afternoon
