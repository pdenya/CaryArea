The Cary Theater's Summer Family Series is the cheapest air conditioning in town: family movies for around $2 a ticket, with shows July 8–10 at 2pm and 7pm, plus more on Saturday the 11th.

The 2pm showtime is the whole point this week. The plan is to hide from the sun between noon and 5, and this puts seven people in a cool, dark theater for the price of a single fancy coffee. It's downtown Cary, so it chains easily with an evening at Downtown Cary Park afterward, and the 7pm shows work as a low-key night out too. When the heat index is screaming, this is the escape hatch.

**Know before you go**

- Shows Jul 8–10 at 2pm and 7pm; additional showtimes Sat Jul 11
- Around $2 per ticket — cheap enough to take the whole crew twice
- The 2pm slot is the ideal peak-heat hideout
