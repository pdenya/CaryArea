You don't have to drive to Jordan Lake to get the family on the water. Bond Park's boathouse rents pedal boats and kayaks for $8 an hour, right in Cary — close enough that it barely counts as an outing, cheap enough that everyone can pick their own vessel. Pedal boats for the giggling-kids contingent, kayaks for anyone feeling independent.

It's first-come, first-served, so there's nothing to book — but there is a schedule trap to dodge. Monday through Thursday the boathouse only operates 9am to 2pm, so a weekday paddle has to be a morning plan, which happens to suit the heat strategy perfectly anyway. Friday and Saturday it runs 9am to 7pm, opening up the golden-hour evening paddle option.

**Know before you go**

- Pedal boats and kayaks, $8/hr
- First-come, first-served — no reservations
- Mon–Thu: 9am–2pm only; Fri–Sat: 9am–7pm
