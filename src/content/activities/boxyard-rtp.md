Feeding a big group with kids of different ages is a logistics problem, and Boxyard RTP is the cheat code: a food hall built from shipping containers where everyone orders exactly what they want and nobody has to agree on a restaurant. It's close to Cary, so it slots in as an easy dinner at the end of almost any day on this list.

There's usually more than food happening, too — live music or trivia most weeks — so a quick dinner can stretch into an actual evening if the group's having fun. Since the container-yard setup is open-air, treat it like your other outdoor picks: this is an after-the-sun-relents dinner spot, not a lunch stop.

**Know before you go**
- Container food hall — everyone picks their own dinner
- Live music or trivia most weeks
- Close to Cary; easy end to any day trip
- Go in the evening during the heat wave
