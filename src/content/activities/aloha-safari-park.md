A rescue animal park where you can pet goats and alpacas, and the headline act is feeding their giraffe for one dollar. Everything is rescues, so it feels good. ~$15 per person, and it's the kind of hands-on animal experience that works great for the 9 and 11-year-olds.

About an hour from Cary, so it's a day trip commitment. Go in the morning to beat the heat — it's mostly outdoor.

**Know before you go**

- ~1 hour from Cary (Cameron, NC)
- ~$15 per person
- Closed Tuesdays — any other day works
- Mostly outdoor, so morning is best
- All rescue animals
