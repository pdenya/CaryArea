Here's the flex move of the week: instead of finding a patch of beach big enough for everyone, rent the whole boat. Crosswinds Boating Center on Jordan Lake has pontoons sized to fit a big group at once — adults, teens, and kids together — with nobody left counting seats.

Four weekday hours on the water runs roughly $160–225, which split across the group is a shockingly reasonable price for a private floating party barge. You set the pace: cruise, anchor, swim off the back, snack, repeat. On a week where the sun is the enemy, being surrounded by lake with a breeze of your own making is about as good as it gets. Call ahead to lock in your boat.

**Know before you go**

- Pontoons seat groups of 7–8
- ~$160–225 for 4 hours on a weekday
- Reserve by phone: (919) 387-7011
- About 25 minutes from Cary
