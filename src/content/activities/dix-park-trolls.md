Danish artist Thomas Dambo installed five giant recycled-wood troll sculptures scattered across Dorothea Dix Park in October 2025. Each one is hidden in a different spot — two near the dog park, two by the cemetery, one near Gipson Play Plaza. It's part art installation, part scavenger hunt, and kids go absolutely nuts for it.

Free, open dawn to dusk, and the trolls have a roughly 10-year lifespan so they'll be there. Combine with the Gipson Play Plaza and/or the Dix Park Car Showcase (Sat 7/11) for a full Dix Park morning.

**Know before you go**

- FREE — just show up and explore
- Bring water and sunscreen, some trolls are in open areas
- Best as an early morning or evening activity in July heat
- Great photo ops — each troll has its own personality
