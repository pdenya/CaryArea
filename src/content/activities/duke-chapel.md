Duke Chapel is the postcard shot of the Triangle — a soaring Gothic tower that makes even jaded teens look up. It anchors a lovely campus walk, and since your week is all about mornings and evenings anyway, its odd summer schedule actually fits your rhythm.

That schedule is the thing to know: during your visit, weekday access doesn't start until 5:30pm (running to 10pm), while weekends open at 8am. So the play is either an evening walk — the tower at dusk is not a bad consolation prize for the heat — or a Saturday morning visit paired with Sarah P. Duke Gardens next door. One more wrinkle: window maintenance runs through July 17, so you'll enter through a side door.

**Know before you go**
- Weekdays: 5:30–10pm only. Weekends: 8am–10pm
- Window maintenance through Jul 17 — enter by the side door
- Best plan: evening walk, or Sat morning combined with Duke Gardens
