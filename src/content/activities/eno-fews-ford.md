This one has a built-in kid magnet: a suspension bridge they will absolutely love. The Cox Mountain route is 3.75 miles with one real climb — good for teens and tweens — while the Buckquarter Creek loop is a mellower 1.5 miles if you want everyone smiling at the end. Either way, the classic shallow swimming hole by the Piper-Cox house is the reward, so pack water shoes.

Start between 8 and 9:30am or after 5:30pm to dodge the heat. Then drive to Maple View Farm for ice cream on the porch — there are rocking chairs, and you will not want to leave.

**Know before you go**

- Cox Mountain: 3.75 mi with one real climb; Buckquarter Creek loop: 1.5 mi
- Free entry
- Check ncparks.gov for flood reroutes
- Do tick checks afterward
