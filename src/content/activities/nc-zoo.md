It's on the list because it's the NC Zoo and someone will suggest it — but honest advice for this particular week: probably skip it. The zoo is mostly outdoor, and pairing a 75-minute drive each way with hours of walking in brutal sun is a recipe for a wilted, cranky crew by noon.

If the group is dead-set on it anyway, treat it like every other outdoor pick this week: go at the coolest part of the day, load up on water and sunscreen, and don't try to see everything. Otherwise, save it for a cooler visit and spend this week's zoo energy on the Lemur Center or the Museum of Life and Science instead.

**Know before you go**

- Admission ~$15–19 per adult
- ~75-minute drive to Asheboro (4401 Zoo Pkwy)
- Mostly outdoor — the official family recommendation this week is: skip
