Saturday morning is farmers market time, and you've got three to choose from on July 11, all running 8am to noon. The Cary Downtown market is the easy pick — it pairs directly with Downtown Cary Park, so the kids can hit the splash pad and Kidzone while the adults browse. Western Wake Farmers Market in Morrisville is the close second option. And if you want to make a morning of it, the Durham Farmers' Market at Durham Central Park pairs with a treat run to The Parlour or Cocoa Cinnamon.

Markets are the right call for the last morning of the visit: they start at 8, when it's still bearable outside, and everyone's back in the AC before the heat peaks.

**Know before you go**

- All three run Sat Jul 11, 8am–noon
- Cary Downtown pairs with Downtown Cary Park; Durham pairs with Parlour/Cocoa Cinnamon
- Go early — by noon you want to be done with outdoor time
- Bring cash and a tote; graze your way through breakfast
