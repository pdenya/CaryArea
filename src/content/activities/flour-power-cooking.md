Flour Power runs kids cooking classes, camps, and family classes at their Cary Parkside location. Pick from 4, 6, or 8 class packages, or look for single-session family classes during the visit week.

This is the kind of activity that produces something tangible — everyone cooks, everyone eats what they made. Works great for the 9 and 11-year-olds especially, and full AC.

**Know before you go**

- 1128 Parkside Main St, Cary
- Kids classes, camps, and family classes available
- Check schedule for availability during visit week
- Full AC kitchen studio
