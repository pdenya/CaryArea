An escape room might be the single best group-size match going: with a group of 6–7, you fill a private room, so it's just your crew versus the clock — no strangers, no waiting on someone else's team. You've got two nearby options: Cipher Escape in Morrisville or The Amazing Escape right in Cary.

Ages skew around 10 and up, which puts tweens squarely in the game and the teens in their element. There's something great about watching the kids crack a puzzle the adults were stuck on — and an hour of frantic, air-conditioned teamwork is exactly what a 95-degree afternoon calls for.

**Know before you go**

- About $28–32 per person
- A group of 6–7 gets a private room to itself
- Ages roughly 10 and up
- Two locations to pick from: Cipher Escape (Morrisville) or The Amazing Escape (Cary)
