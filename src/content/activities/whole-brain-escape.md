Award-winning escape rooms designed by video game professionals in downtown Apex. The puzzles are a cut above the typical escape room — more creative, better production value. Family-friendly rooms available.

A good alternative to Cipher Escape (Morrisville) if you're staying close to Apex that day, and the downtown Apex location means you can pair it with Salem Street shops and ice cream after.

**Know before you go**

- 410 Upchurch St, downtown Apex
- ~$28–35 per person
- Book ahead — don't just show up
- Family-friendly rooms available
- Pairs perfectly with Downtown Apex stroll
