Wheels is Durham's everything-park: roller skating, go-karts, mini golf, and batting cages all in one spot, each running about $7–8. That à-la-carte pricing is great for a mixed-age crew — the teens can run karts and batting cages while the younger two skate and putt, and nobody pays for attractions they won't use.

The scheduling fine print is what you need to watch. On weekdays the park mostly runs evening sessions, and it's closed on Thursday — which happens to be your beach-overnight day anyway, so no great loss. For your week, that makes Wheels a natural evening outing, which suits the heat strategy perfectly: arrive after the worst of the sun, and let everyone pick their poison.

**Know before you go**
- Attractions ~$7–8 each: skating, go-karts, mini golf, batting cages
- Weekdays are mostly evening sessions
- Closed Thursday
