This is the one to build a whole morning around. The wooded 2.2-mile loop is a manageable distance for everyone from grade-schoolers to teens, and the back side delivers the goods: a pond and wading in the Eno to cool everybody off mid-hike.

Then run the local combo, in order: hike in the morning, lunch at El Restaurante Ixtapa, stroll around Hillsborough, and finish at Whit's Frozen Custard. It's the rare plan where the hike is arguably the third-best part. The lot is small, so be there by 9 — which conveniently lands you in the 8–9:30am window locals recommend for beating the heat anyway (or go after 5:30pm).

**Know before you go**

- 2.2-mile wooded loop; pond and Eno wading on the back side
- Free; small lot — arrive by 9
- Pairs with Ixtapa lunch, downtown Hillsborough, and Whit's Frozen Custard
- Do tick checks afterward
