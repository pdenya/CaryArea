Baby goats, alpacas, highland cows, and gem mining all in one stop. It's the kind of farm visit where the younger kids lose their minds over the animals and everyone ends up staying longer than planned.

Close to Chapel Hill, so it pairs well with Morehead Planetarium or a Franklin Street lunch. Mostly outdoor — go in the morning.

**Know before you go**

- Verify July hours at springhavenfarm.com before going
- Mostly outdoor, so morning visit is best
- Pairs well with Chapel Hill stops (Morehead Planetarium, Franklin St)
