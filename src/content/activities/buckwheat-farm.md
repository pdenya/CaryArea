There's something about sending kids down a row of blueberry bushes with a bucket that works on every age — the nine-year-old grazes, the teens compete, and everybody comes home with dessert. Buckwheat Farm on Holland Road is the closest picking option to home, which matters when you need to be done and back in the AC before the sun gets serious.

One big catch: mid-July is the tail end of blueberry season, so this trip lives or dies on a phone call. Ring the farm at 919-303-0339 before you load the car. If they're picked out, Vollmer Farm in Bunn is the better mid-July bet. The plan pencils this in for Wednesday morning — go early, pick fast, get out.

**Know before you go**
- Open Mon–Sat, 9am–6pm
- Call first: 919-303-0339 — tail of the season
- 2700 Holland Rd, Apex — the closest farm to home
- Morning only in this heat
