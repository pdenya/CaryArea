A real sandy beach twenty-some minutes from Cary, for the price of a fancy coffee. Ebenezer Church Access on Jordan Lake gives you a roped-off swim area for the kids, a playground for when they inexplicably want a break from the water, and showers to rinse the lake off before the drive back.

For a big group, $4 per car is about as cheap as a beach day gets — pack a cooler and you've built an entire morning for pocket change. The one catch: there are no lifeguards, so this is a swim-where-you-can-see-them situation. In a heat wave, treat it as a morning outing and be heading home by lunch.

**Know before you go**

- $4 per car
- Gates open 8am–9pm
- Sandy beach, roped swim area, playground, showers on site
- No lifeguards — supervise swimmers
- Go in the morning to beat the heat
- Address: 2582 Beaver Creek Rd, Apex
