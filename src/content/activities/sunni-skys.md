Over 100 homemade flavors under one roof — that's the draw that gets people driving out to Angier for ice cream. With four kids in the group, just reading the flavor board is half the entertainment. And then there's the legend: "Cold Sweat," the pepper ice cream that exists purely so teenagers can dare each other to order it. Yours will.

It's a destination scoop shop, the kind of quirky detour that becomes the story everyone retells later — assuming the dare goes badly enough to be funny.

**Know before you go**

- Cash only — hit an ATM first
- 100+ homemade flavors; the Cold Sweat pepper flavor is the teen dare
- Heads up: there are ex-employee allegations about the owner — your call on whether to go
