Boxcar is Raleigh's retro arcade — the cabinets the adults grew up on, now with the kids lined up next to them trying to figure out why Dad is suddenly so good at video games. It's a bar-plus-arcade hybrid, which means grown-up drinks for the adults and a wall of classics for everyone.

The scheduling trick matters here: on weekdays, minors are welcome with a parent only until 8pm. That makes this a perfect late-afternoon play — roll in when the heat is peaking, game hard for a couple of hours in the AC, and clear out around dinner time with quarters spent and bragging rights settled.

**Know before you go**

- Minors OK with a parent until 8pm on weekdays — build the visit around that
- Late afternoon is the sweet spot: peak heat outside, arcade inside
