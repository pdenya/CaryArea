Urban Air in Apex is the more little-kid-friendly alternative to DEFY Raleigh. More variety of attractions — trampolines, climbing walls, obstacle courses — with sections scaled for younger kids. Good option if the 9 and 11-year-olds are the priority.

**Know before you go**

- ~$20–30 per person
- More little-kid-friendly than DEFY
- Full AC — great midday activity
- Right in Apex, close to home
