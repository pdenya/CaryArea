Wake County libraries are running "Unearth a Story" summer programming all week — free performances, crafts, and activities at Cary branches. It's a zero-planning, zero-cost AC escape that works especially well for the younger kids when you need a low-key afternoon.

County parks nature programs are sporadic this summer, so the library system is actually the better bet for free structured activities.

**Know before you go**

- Completely free
- Check wake.gov/library-events for specific branch schedules
- Multiple Cary branches, so pick the closest one
- Air-conditioned — perfect midday filler
