Confirmed Friday 7/10: Late Models, Legends, and Bandoleros on the quarter-mile bullring. Gates open around 5–6pm, racing starts around 7. It's exactly the kind of loud, accessible motorsport evening that works for the whole crew — real cars, real racing, very family-friendly atmosphere.

**Know before you go**

- Gates ~5–6pm, racing ~7pm
- ~$10–15 admission
- Bring ear protection for the younger kids
- Evening event, so the heat isn't a factor
