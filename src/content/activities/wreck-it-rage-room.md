Sometimes the best family bonding is handing everyone safety gear and letting them demolish things together. Wreck It Rage Room in Durham lets you do exactly that — smash, shatter, and swing away in air-conditioned, gloriously destructive comfort.

The age math is friendly: it's open to ages 8 and up with an adult, which means even the younger kids clear the bar. It's the kind of outing the teens will pretend to be too cool for right up until they're mid-swing, and the younger kids will talk about for the rest of the week.

**Know before you go**

- Runs about $25–45 per person
- Ages 8+ with an adult along
- Alternative: iSmash in Wake Forest also has a paint-splatter room, open to ages 3+
