When the afternoon sun turns brutal, Frankie's is the classic escape hatch: a big indoor-outdoor fun park where walking in the door costs nothing and you pay only for what you actually play. That makes it easy to scale to your crew — the younger kids can burn through a couple of attractions while the teens pick their own, and nobody's locked into an expensive all-day pass.

It also happens to have the best mini golf near Cary, so if putt-putt is on the wish list this week, you can check that box here instead of making a separate trip.

**Know before you go**

- 11190 Fun Park Dr, Raleigh
- Free entry — attractions run about $8–12 each
- Open Mon–Thu 12pm–11pm, so it works for a late-evening outing after the heat breaks
