Any Durham outing — the gardens, the museums, the American Tobacco Campus — deserves a treat stop bolted on, and Durham has a deep bench. The Parlour is the ice cream anchor, open noon to 10pm, so it works after a morning adventure or as an evening finale. Cocoa Cinnamon covers the grown-ups' coffee needs, Pincho Loco brings its own ice cream style to the rotation, and Locopops are the frozen pops built for exactly this kind of weather.

When you want to linger instead of grab-and-go, Ponysaurus is the play: a big lawn plus games, and it's kid-welcome — the adults get a relaxed evening while all four kids burn off the sugar.

**Know before you go**

- The Parlour: ice cream, noon–10pm
- Cocoa Cinnamon, Pincho Loco, and Locopops round out the crawl
- Ponysaurus: big lawn + games, kid-welcome — best for hanging out
