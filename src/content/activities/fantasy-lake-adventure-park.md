Picture a giant floating obstacle course — a Wibit inflatable park — bobbing on a quarry lake, and picture your whole crew scrambling, bouncing, and wiping out on it for two straight hours. That's Fantasy Lake, and it's the kind of outing the kids will still be talking about at Christmas.

It's built for exactly this kind of crew: ages 3 and up, so everyone from the youngest kids to the teens (and any adults with something to prove) is in. At $40 a person for a two-hour session it's the splurge tier of the water list, but it's a genuinely memorable one. The catch is demand — July sessions sell out, so this is not a wake-up-and-wing-it plan.

**Know before you go**

- $40 per person for a 2-hour session
- Ages 3+
- Buy passes and complete waivers online in advance — July sessions sell out
- About 30 minutes away in Wake Forest
