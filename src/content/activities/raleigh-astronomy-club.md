The Raleigh Astronomy Club runs "Second Friday Under the Stars" on July 10 at 8:30pm — free, all ages, telescopes provided. You just show up. Fireflies are still active at dusk that week, so the younger kids have something to chase while the stargazers set up.

It's weather-dependent — check raleighastro.org the day of. If it's clear, this is a genuinely magical free evening that doesn't compete with the heat at all.

**Know before you go**

- Free, all ages, no registration needed
- Telescopes provided — just show up
- Weather-dependent: check raleighastro.org day-of
- 8:30pm start, so the heat is long gone
