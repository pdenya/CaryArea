If you want one loud, cold, everybody-wins afternoon, Dave & Buster's is it. The arcade floor is the great equalizer for your crew — the younger kids and the teens will all find games they're obsessed with, and the adults get to be kids too. Best of all, it's right on Walnut Street in Cary, so it's a five-alarm heat escape without a real drive.

It's not the cheapest item on the list, but there's a smart way to play it: Wednesdays typically bring half-price games, which turns a splurge into a solid deal.

**Know before you go**

- 1111 Walnut St, Cary
- Budget roughly $25–40 per person
- Wednesday is typically half-price games — the day to go this week
