Free-roam VR is the good kind — no wires, no sitting in a chair, just headsets on and the whole group walking around inside the same virtual escape room together. Xcape Reality brings that experience right to Cary, and with an age floor around 8, everyone from the grade-schoolers up to the adults can play on the same team.

It's the rare activity where the age gap between kids genuinely disappears: solving puzzles in VR, a tween is every bit as useful as a teenager. And if you were holding out for Sandbox VR or Zero Latency — those don't exist in the Triangle (Charlotte only), so this is the local play.

**Know before you go**

- Around $30–40 per person
- Ages roughly 8 and up
- Fully indoors — a solid peak-afternoon escape from the heat
