Fenton is the low-effort crowd-pleaser: an open-air district right in Cary where everyone can wander to their own happy place. The teens get Colletta pizza (officially teen-approved), the kids get Honeysuckle Gelato, and anyone who needs a quiet ten minutes gets the bookstore. It's the kind of place where an "easy dinner" turns into a full evening without anyone noticing.

The bonus this week: Fenton Square runs free events on several of your days — Thursday 7/9 in the morning, Saturday 7/11, and Tuesday and Wednesday evenings at 6:30pm. Time your visit around one of those and dinner comes with entertainment. Being outdoors, it's an evening play during the heat wave — arrive as the sun drops, gelato in hand.

**Know before you go**
- Free Fenton Square events: Thu 7/9 morning, Sat 7/11, and Tue/Wed at 6:30pm
- Honeysuckle Gelato, Colletta pizza, and a bookstore on site
- Evening visits only in this heat
