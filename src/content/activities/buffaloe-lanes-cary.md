Sometimes the winning move in a heat wave is the most obvious one: a couple of hours of bowling in serious air conditioning. Buffaloe Lanes on High House Road is the family-focused pick — close by, cheap per game, and an easy activity where a grade-schooler with bumpers and a competitive teen can share the same lane and still both have fun.

At $5–7 a game plus shoe rental, it's one of the most budget-friendly afternoons on this whole list, and there's zero booking or planning required.

**Know before you go**

- 151 High House Rd, Cary
- Roughly $5–7 per game, plus shoes
- Alternative: Bowlero Cary runs $15.99 unlimited bowling 11am–1pm on weekdays — the better deal if your crew wants to bowl for hours
