This is the rare stop that solves the whole age-spread problem in one parking spot. Marbles is pure hands-on heaven for grade-schoolers and tweens — the kind of climb-on-everything, touch-everything play museum that eats an afternoon whole. Meanwhile, the teens can peel off to the IMAX right next door and catch something on the giant screen instead of trailing behind the little kids.

At around $9 a head for the museum, it's one of the cheaper indoor-AC afternoons on the list, and downtown Raleigh means food options are steps away when everyone comes out hungry.

**Know before you go**

- Museum admission runs ~$9
- Split the crew: younger kids in Marbles, teens at the IMAX next door
- Fully indoor and air-conditioned — a safe peak-heat pick
