ComedyWorx runs all-ages improv shows Thursday, Friday, and Saturday at 8pm. It's cheap, it's in the AC, and improv is the kind of thing that works for a mixed-age group — the 9-year-old laughs at different stuff than the teens, and everyone has a good time.

Friday at 10pm is the mature show — skip that one with kids. The 8pm shows are the family-friendly ones.

**Know before you go**

- ~$12–15 per person
- 8pm shows are all-ages; avoid the 10pm Friday show
- Air-conditioned — great afternoon-heat alternative
