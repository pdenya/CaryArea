The Candle Bar and Paddywax are both at Brightleaf Square in Durham — pick your scents, pour your own candle, take it home. Walk-ins welcome. AR Workshop in Raleigh is another option. It's a teen+tween+adult activity that actually works, and everyone leaves with a souvenir.

Full AC, so it's a perfect midday activity. About an hour from choosing scents to finished candle.

**Know before you go**

- The Candle Bar + Paddywax at Brightleaf Square (Durham)
- AR Workshop (Raleigh) as an alternative
- Walk-ins OK — no reservation needed
- ~$30–45 per person depending on vessel size
- Everyone takes a candle home
