Here's the quiet genius move of the whole week: the YMCA of the Triangle offers a free 7-day trial pass, and the Taylor Family YMCA right in Cary has pools. A visit week is exactly the legitimate use case this trial exists for — your guests are in town for less than a week, the trial runs seven days, and the math writes itself.

That turns "we need somewhere to swim" into a solved problem for the entire visit, at a price of exactly nothing. It's the kind of hack that feels almost too easy: real pools, real facilities, no admission fees stacking up across a big group, and a reliable indoor-adjacent fallback for any afternoon when the outdoor plans wilt in the heat. Sign up, swim all week, high-five yourself on Saturday.

**Know before you go**

- YMCA of the Triangle offers a free 7-day trial pass
- Taylor Family YMCA in Cary has pools
- Legit for a visit week
