If the week deserves one full-throttle water park day, this is it. Wet'n Wild Emerald Pointe is the real deal — a whole day of slides and splashing that can absorb a big group with wildly different thrill tolerances, about an hour up the road in Greensboro.

The reason it's flagged in red ink: the online promo, up to 45% off, ends July 6 — so this is a decide-now situation. Buying Sunday or Monday turns a ~$61–65 gate price into a genuine bargain. Once you're in, play it smart: Tuesday and Wednesday crowds are much lighter, and the park is all concrete, so arrive at open before the pavement turns into a griddle.

**Know before you go**

- Gate price ~$61–65; online promo up to 45% off ends 7/6 — buy before the deadline
- Open daily, roughly 10am–6pm
- Tue/Wed crowds are much lighter
- All-concrete park — arrive at open to beat the heat
- About a 1-hour drive from Cary
