Floating a river on a tube is the platonic ideal of beating a heat wave, and Saxapahaw's stretch of the Haw is your closest shot at it. It's a self-guided rental with a minimum age of 5, so every kid in this crew clears the bar, and it pairs naturally with the Saxapahaw day trip if you want to make an afternoon of the river-mill village.

Know what you're signing up for, though: there's no shuttle. This is a tube-and-hike arrangement — you float down, then walk your tubes back upstream, which is either a dealbreaker or part of the adventure depending on your crew's mood. And since July rivers can run fickle, call ahead about water levels before you commit the drive.

**Know before you go**

- Self-guided rental, minimum age 5
- No shuttle — walk tubes back upstream
- Call ahead about July water levels
- About 50 minutes away; pairs with a Saxapahaw day trip
