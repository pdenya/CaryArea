Raven Rock is genuinely great: a shaded loop leading to a 130-step staircase that drops you at the base of the big rock itself, with a little Cape Fear River beach as the payoff. The kids would love the staircase going down. The problem is the staircase going back up — 130 steps in 90-degree heat, at the end of an hour's drive each way.

The honest verdict from the research: save this one for a cooler week. If the forecast breaks and you go anyway, follow the same playbook as everywhere else — be on the trail between 8 and 9:30am or after 5:30pm, and bring more water than you think you need.

**Know before you go**

- About an hour's drive from Cary; free entry
- Shaded loop, then 130 steps down to the rock and river beach — and 130 back up
- Recommendation: skip during the heat wave; save for a cooler week
- Do tick checks afterward
