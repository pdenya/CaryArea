Mini golf is a staycation staple, but in a heat wave the classic outdoor course is a sweaty slog. Moonwalk Mini Golf at Galaxy Fun Park solves that: an indoor blacklight course where everything glows, the AC hums, and nobody's begging for shade by hole four.

The glow-in-the-dark angle gives it just enough novelty that the teens won't roll their eyes, while the younger two get the full neon spectacle. And if anyone's wondering — there's no Monster Mini Golf in the Triangle, so this is the blacklight course to beat. At roughly $10–12 a round, it's an easy, cheap way to eat a chunk of a scorching afternoon.

**Know before you go**

- Around $10–12 per person for the blacklight course
- Fully indoors and air-conditioned — a true peak-heat pick
