This is a real animal rescue with lions, tigers, and wolves — not a zoo, not a petting farm. You get a guided 90-minute tour through the facility, and the animals are close. It's the kind of thing kids remember for years.

The catch: **weekend tours only**, which means Saturday July 11 is the only option. And summer Saturdays close 1–4pm for the animals' sake in the heat, so you need a **morning tour**. Reservations are required — book now.

**Know before you go**

- ~55 minutes from Cary (Burlington)
- Weekend only = Sat 7/11
- Book a morning tour — they close afternoons in summer
- Reservations required, no walk-ins
