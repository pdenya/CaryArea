Sushi for a big group sounds like a budget disaster — until you find the place with buy-one-get-one rolls. Sushi Iwa in Apex runs BOGO on rolls, which turns a big-family sushi night from a splurge into cheap fun. Order a pile, pass the plates around, let the kids get adventurous when the second roll is free anyway.

If you were dreaming of a conveyor-belt sushi experience (the kind where the kids grab plates off a moving track), sorry: Kura hasn't made it to North Carolina yet. BOGO rolls at a table are the next best thing, and honestly easier to manage with a group this size.

**Know before you go**

- BOGO rolls — the whole reason it's on the list
- No Kura conveyor-belt sushi in NC yet, so this is the value play
- Close by in Apex
