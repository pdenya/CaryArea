When four kids have been cooped up in the AC for days and are starting to bounce off the actual walls, take them somewhere with walls built for bouncing. DEFY Raleigh is a trampoline and ninja park — wall-to-wall trampolines plus obstacle-course challenges that give the teens something to conquer and the younger two something to attempt heroically.

Two hours here is the closest thing to a guaranteed early bedtime this week offers. It's indoor, it's air-conditioned, and it converts an unusable scorching afternoon into pure burned-off energy. If your group skews younger on a given day, note the alternative below.

**Know before you go**

- Roughly $25–30 for a 2-hour session
- Alternative: Urban Air in Apex has more little-kid-friendly attractions if the younger ones are driving the choice
