Salem Street in downtown Apex earned the title of best evening stroll in the research, and it's not hard to see why: a walkable historic main street stacked with treats. FRESH. Local Ice Cream and Mama Bird's cover the cone department, Kilwins handles the fudge, and the whole thing moves at exactly the pace a mixed group of kids, teens, and adults wants to move after dinner.

The secret weapon is Salem Pond Park, just a block away — a pond loop, a playground, and ducks, which is precisely the trio a nine-year-old and an eleven-year-old need to burn off the sugar. Wait for the evening cool-down, park once, and let the whole night unfold on foot.

**Know before you go**
- FRESH. Local Ice Cream, Mama Bird's, and Kilwins fudge all on Salem Street
- Salem Pond Park is one block away: pond loop, playground, ducks
- Evening stroll — skip the midday heat
