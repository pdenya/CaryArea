When it's pushing triple digits outside, there's a certain deep satisfaction in strapping on skates and gliding around a literal sheet of ice. Polar Ice Cary is the coldest option on this entire list, which makes it the perfect peak-heat pick — schedule it for the worst afternoon of the week and enjoy the whiplash.

It's also a bargain: weekday skating is $10 with rental skates included, right on Buck Jones Road. The whole crew can wobble, race, and warm up with hot chocolate energy in July.

**Know before you go**

- 1410 Buck Jones Rd
- Weekdays $10, skate rental included
- Check the polaricecary.com calendar for midday summer public sessions before you head over
- The coldest indoor option around — bring a light layer, seriously
