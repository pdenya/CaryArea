Game On has 21 unique escape rooms across 5 NC locations, plus VR escape room experiences powered by Ubisoft. The Cary location is at 107 Edinburgh South Dr. The VR option is a nice twist if the group has already done a traditional escape room earlier in the week.

**Know before you go**

- 107 Edinburgh South Dr, Suite 203 & 206, Cary
- ~$28–35 per person
- Traditional escape rooms + VR escape games
- Book ahead
