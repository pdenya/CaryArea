ShopSpace runs forging intro classes in Raleigh, and the standout is a **parent-child class for ages 10–13** — a rare fit for the 11-year-old niece. Actual blacksmithing: heating metal, hammering it into shape, taking home what you make.

Check shopspace.org/classes for that week's schedule. This is the kind of unique maker experience that sticks — way more memorable than another round of bowling.

**Know before you go**

- Check shopspace.org/classes for availability that week
- Parent-child class: ages 10–13 (niece qualifies)
- Regular forging intro for teens and adults
- Book ahead — class sizes are small
