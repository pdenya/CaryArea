Never underestimate a good mall day when it's scorching outside. Streets at Southpoint is the Triangle's big one, and it's a genuinely easy win for a mixed-age crew: the teens can peel off and do their own thing, the younger two can browse with the adults, and everyone stays in air conditioning without anyone needing a ticket or a reservation.

The real move is the pairing — Topgolf sits right next door, so you can turn a lazy mall afternoon into a full outing without a second drive.

**Know before you go**

- 6910 Fayetteville Rd, Durham
- Open Mon–Thu 11am–7pm
- Free to wander — spend as much or as little as you like
- Pair it with Topgolf next door for a two-stop afternoon
