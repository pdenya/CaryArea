If the kids have energy to burn and scores to settle, Xtreme Park Adventures in Durham is the place to settle them. The trick that makes it work for a mixed-age crew: gel blasters are gentle enough for younger kids, and paintball is open to ages 8 and up — so nobody has to sit on the sidelines while the teens have all the fun.

The catch is that it's outdoors, and this is a heat-wave week. That makes this a strictly morning mission: get out there early, run your battles before the sun turns brutal, and be back in the AC by lunch with everyone splattered and satisfied.

**Know before you go**

- Gel blasters work for younger kids; paintball is ages 8+
- Morning slot only in this heat — do not attempt a midday session
