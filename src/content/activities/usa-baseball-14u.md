The USA Baseball 14U National Championships take over Thomas Brooks Park in Cary from July 6–10 — elite youth ball, right in town, for free or close to it. These are some of the best 14-year-old players in the country, which makes it legitimately fun to watch even if nobody in your group follows baseball.

It made the list because it solves the hardest slot of a heat-wave week: the evening. Games run into the evening hours, admission is free or cheap, and it's a no-reservation, show-up-when-you-want outing you can slot into almost any night before the beach overnight. With the Durham Bulls away all week, this is the live-baseball fix.

**Know before you go**

- Runs Jul 6–10 at Thomas Brooks Park, Cary
- Free or cheap admission; evening games beat the heat
- Zero booking required — a flexible filler for any open night
- Durham Bulls are away all week, so this is the local baseball option
