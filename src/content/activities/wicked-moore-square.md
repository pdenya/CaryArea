Friday night, downtown Raleigh: a completely free outdoor screening of "Wicked: For Good" at Moore Square, part of the NCMA series. Food trucks roll in at 6pm, the film starts at 7:15, and by then the evening has taken the edge off the heat. Blankets on the grass, dinner from a truck, a big movie under the sky — that's a hard combo for kids and teens alike to turn down.

One catch: it goes head-to-head with the Sidecar Social Club show at Koka Booth the same night, so you've got a Friday decision to make. If your crowd skews toward the younger kids, the movie probably wins.

**Know before you go**

- Fri Jul 10 — food trucks at 6pm, film at 7:15pm, all free
- Bring blankets or low chairs for the lawn
- Competes with the Koka Booth concert that night — pick one
- Evening timing fits the week's heat strategy perfectly
