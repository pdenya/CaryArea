There's something about a roller rink that works for every age at once — the younger kids clinging to the wall, the tweens racing laps, the teens pretending to be too cool and then skating for two hours straight. United Skates in Raleigh is the spot now that Jellybeans in Cary has permanently closed.

The scheduling quirk is the whole game here: on weekdays, public sessions run evenings only. That actually fits this week perfectly — it slots into the after-dinner window when you'd want to be out of the sun anyway.

**Know before you go**

- 2901 Trawick Rd, Raleigh
- About $8–12 admission plus skate rental
- Weekday public sessions are evenings only: Mon 6–8:30pm, Wed/Thu 7–10pm
- Jellybeans Cary is permanently closed — don't bother heading there
