The Devil Dogz play Coastal Plain League ball at Five County Stadium (the old Mudcats park) in Zebulon. Two games land during the visit week, and one of them is a gift: **Thursday 10:30am** — a morning game that beats the worst of the heat.

Saturday's game is the more traditional 6:30pm evening start. Either way, it's cheap, casual summer ball with all the minor-league-park trimmings.

**Know before you go**

- ~30 minutes from Cary
- Thu 7/9 at 10:30am is the rare morning option
- Sat 7/11 at 6:30pm for an evening game
- ~$10–13 admission
- The Salamanders are away all week, so this is the closer-to-home baseball option
