Go-karting outdoors in this heat would be misery — go-karting indoors is a highlight of the week. Rush Hour Karting puts real racing in real air conditioning, and it's tailor-made for a group with a competitive streak: teens and adults in the fast karts, the younger racers in juniors, and bragging rights on the line at the finish.

The Morrisville location is the closer one. There's also a Garner location (5335 Raynor Rd) if that's more convenient for the day's plan.

The height rule is the thing to check before you promise anyone anything — junior karts require riders to be both ages 8–14 and at least 48 inches tall, so measure the younger kids at home first.

**Know before you go**

- **Morrisville** (closer to Cary) or **Garner** (5335 Raynor Rd)
- $25 per race, or 3 races for $60
- Junior karts: ages 8–14 AND 48"+ tall — younger kids qualify if they clear 48"
- Adult karts are 15+, so the teens race with the grown-ups
