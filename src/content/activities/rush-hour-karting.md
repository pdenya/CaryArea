Go-karting outdoors in this heat would be misery — go-karting indoors is a highlight of the week. Rush Hour Karting in Garner puts real racing in real air conditioning, and it's tailor-made for a group with a competitive streak: teens and adults in the fast karts, the younger racers in juniors, and bragging rights on the line at the finish.

The height rule is the thing to check before you promise anyone anything — junior karts require riders to be both ages 8–14 and at least 48 inches tall, so measure the younger kids at home first.

**Know before you go**

- 5335 Raynor Rd, Garner
- $25 per race, or 3 races for $60
- Junior karts: ages 8–14 AND 48"+ tall — younger kids qualify if they clear 48"
- Adult karts are 15+, so the teens race with the grown-ups
