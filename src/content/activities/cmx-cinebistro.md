A dark theater during the hottest hours of the day is the oldest trick in the summer playbook, and CinéBistro is the plush version: big recliners and food delivered right to your seat, so a matinee doubles as lunch. It's in Cary at Waverly Place, which means minimal driving for maximum cool-down.

The one catch is timing — this is a matinee outing for your group, not an evening one, since after-dark showings often go 21+. Plan it as the peak-heat afternoon block and you're golden.

**Know before you go**

- 525 New Waverly Pl, Cary
- Tickets run about $13.50–16, with in-seat dining
- Evening shows are often 21+; matinees are family-OK — verify before booking
- Alternative: Alamo Drafthouse Raleigh is the family-friendlier option at $8–11.50
