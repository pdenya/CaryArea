This is the roadside attraction you normally have to drive to the mountains for — except it's right in Cary. Kids work a sluice full of gem-studded dirt, sift out their treasure, and walk away with a pocketful of sparkly loot. There are fossil buckets for the dino-inclined, and goats, because apparently every great hidden gem this week comes with goats.

For grade-schoolers and tweens this is close to a guaranteed hit, and the take-home gems double as souvenirs of the week. It's outdoors, so slot it into a cooler morning or evening window rather than fighting the mid-afternoon sun over a water sluice.

**Know before you go**

- Right in town — one of the shortest drives on the list
- Sluice gem mining plus fossil buckets, plus resident goats
- Outdoor — pick a morning or evening slot during the heat wave
