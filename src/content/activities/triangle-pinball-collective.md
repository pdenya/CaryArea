Tucked inside the Flight Deck bottle shop in Morrisville, the Triangle Pinball Collective is a wall of 20-plus machines — including genuine 70s vintage tables the kids have never seen outside a movie. It's the kind of quirky, low-stakes stop that ends up being everyone's sleeper favorite.

The setup works nicely for a mixed-age crew: the adults grab something from the bottle shop, the kids feed the machines, and everyone drifts between tables comparing high scores. Daytime is the move here — the space is family-OK during the day, so aim for an afternoon session when the sun outside is at its worst and the AC inside is at its best.

**Know before you go**

- 20+ machines, including 70s vintage pinball
- It lives inside Flight Deck, a bottle shop — family-friendly during the daytime
