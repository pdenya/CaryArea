Here's the math that makes hibachi perfect for a big group: fill an entire grill table yourselves, and the onion volcano, the shrimp toss, the whole knife-flipping spectacle is all just for your crew. No sharing the show with strangers, no waiting for another party to fill out the seats. Dinner and entertainment in one air-conditioned sitting, which is exactly what a heat-wave evening calls for.

Kanki has locations in Raleigh and Durham, and it's the classic Triangle hibachi name. If you'd rather not drive as far, Kazu in Cary is the closer option for the same grill-table experience.

**Know before you go**

- A big group fills a whole grill table — a private show
- Figure ~$25–45 per person
- Kazu in Cary is closer if you want to skip the drive
