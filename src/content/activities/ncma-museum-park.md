The NCMA is the art museum for families who think they don't like art museums. The galleries are free and blissfully air-conditioned — a legitimate peak-heat refuge — and the surrounding Museum Park adds a sculpture trail dotted with whirligigs that kids actually want to walk to.

The smart heat-wave play: galleries during the hot hours, whirligig trail when the sun softens. The outdoor sculpture walk is best saved for morning or evening, same as every other outdoor thing this week. Between the two halves you can stretch this into as short or long a visit as the group's attention span allows, all for free.

**Know before you go**

- Free admission to the air-conditioned galleries
- Whirligig sculpture trail is outdoors — do it morning or evening, not mid-afternoon
- Part of the same NCMA orbit as the free "Wicked: For Good" outdoor movie at Moore Square on Fri 7/10
