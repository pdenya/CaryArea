Not every outing needs to be an expedition. Swift Creek Bluffs is a one-mile stroll right in Cary — boardwalk, huge old beech trees, and the "Stairway to Heaven" stairs, which the kids will treat as a challenge and the adults will treat as cardio. The whole thing is a 30–45 minute leg-stretcher, which makes it perfect for the gaps in the week: before dinner, after a lazy pool morning, or when the crew needs to get outside but nobody wants a real hike.

Stick to the smart windows anyway — 8 to 9:30am or after 5:30pm — since even a short walk feels better when the sun isn't at full blast.

**Know before you go**

- 1 mile; plan on 30–45 minutes
- Free
- Boardwalk, Stairway to Heaven stairs, huge old beeches
- Do tick checks afterward
