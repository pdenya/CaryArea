Another paint-your-own pottery option, this one at Waverly Place in Cary. $10 studio fee plus the cost of your piece (from ~$18). Same concept as Amazing Glaze in Apex — pick a piece, paint it, they fire it — but more convenient if your day is already in Cary.

**Know before you go**

- 316 Colonades Way, Suite 215, Cary (Waverly Place)
- $10 studio fee + cost of piece (from ~$18)
- Walk-ins welcome
- Pieces fired and picked up later (or shipped)
