A board game cafe with over 850 games in the library, coffee, and food. Pay $2.50 per person for a library day pass and play as long as you want — kids under 12 are free with an adult. There's also a Morrisville location.

This is the perfect low-key afternoon when the group needs a break from big outings. Everyone finds something they like in a library that deep, and you can camp out for hours in the AC without spending much.

**Know before you go**

- Cary (821 Bass Pro Ln) and Morrisville locations
- $2.50/person library day pass, kids under 12 free
- 850+ games — staff will help you pick
- Coffee, food, and drinks available
