Locals call this hidden gem in the Korstian Division "the closest to feeling like the mountains in the Triangle." Park roadside on Whitfield Road, and it's only about a 15-minute walk down a gravel road to the old concrete bridge, flat rocks, and shallow wading in New Hope Creek. That's the best walk-to-water ratio of any hike on the list — perfect for any kid whose enthusiasm runs out before the water does.

Aim for the trailhead between 8 and 9:30am, or after 5:30pm when things cool off. Heads up: wading here is tolerated rather than official, so keep it low-key.

**Know before you go**

- Free; roadside parking on Whitfield Road fills on weekends
- ~15-minute walk on a gravel road to the creek
- Wading is tolerated, not official
- Do tick checks afterward
