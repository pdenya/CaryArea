Crooked Hammock is a beach-town brewpub concept from Delaware that opened in Raleigh in April 2026. The whole vibe is "backyard cookout" — playground for kids, hammocks to lounge in, fire pits, ping pong, cornhole, ring toss, plus craft beer and a full food menu. Kids menu and mocktails too.

This is the kind of place where the adults relax while the kids play, and everybody's happy. Great for a group dinner that doesn't feel stressful.

**Know before you go**

- 4501 Edwards Mill Rd, Raleigh (GlenLake area)
- Mon–Thu 11am–10pm, Fri–Sat 11am–11pm, Sun 10am–10pm
- Outdoor seating is the draw — go evening when it cools off
- Dog-friendly patio
