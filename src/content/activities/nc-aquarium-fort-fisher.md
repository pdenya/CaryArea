The NC Aquarium at Fort Fisher is 15 minutes south of Carolina Beach — the perfect Friday morning stop on the way home from the beach overnight. Book online ahead of time.

Open 9–5, full AC, and a solid 2-hour visit. Hit it Friday morning after Britt's Donuts, then get on the road by 2pm to beat Raleigh rush hour.

**Know before you go**

- ~$15 adult / $11 child
- 15 minutes south of Carolina Beach
- Book online in advance
- Open 9–5, plan ~2 hours
- Pairs perfectly with the Thu–Fri beach overnight
