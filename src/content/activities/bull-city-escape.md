If your crew catches the escape-room bug — or you just want to start with the best — Bull City Escape is Durham's top escape room. Same great formula: your whole crew locked in together, an hour on the clock, and a room full of puzzles that reward having brains of every age on the team.

It pairs naturally with a Durham day. Knock out the escape room during the worst of the afternoon heat, then you're already positioned for an evening stroll or dinner in town. For a mixed group of teens and younger kids, an hour of shared mission is worth every penny.

**Know before you go**

- About $28–32 per person
- Book ahead — don't count on walking in
- Located in Durham, so plan it as part of a Durham outing
